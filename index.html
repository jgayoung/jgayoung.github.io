Hi! I'm Jennifer.

I am a writer, researcher, and teacher studying Korean diasporic multilingualism and translation, as well as computational methods for literary study.

I am currently a PhD student in Modern Thought and Literature at Stanford, where my work is supported by a Ford Foundation Predoctoral Fellowship. Before moving to California, I lived and worked in New York City, where I grew up and attended Columbia University. I earned my BA in Computer Science and Comparative Literature and Society.
