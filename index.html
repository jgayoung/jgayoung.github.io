Hi! I'm Jennifer.

I am a writer and researcher based in New York City.

You can hear me over on the New Books Network podcast, where I chat with scholars about their research in Asian American studies, digital humanities, and literary studies. I work a day job in tech, and outside my work I help Asian American community organizations better understand Asian voter participation in New York City.
