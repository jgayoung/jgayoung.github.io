New Books Network

  • Min Hyoung Song, Climate Lyricism (2022)
  • Seb Franklin, The Digitally Disposed: Racial Capitalism and the Informatics of Value (2021)
  • Ellen Jones, Literature in Motion: Translating Multilingualism Across the Americas (2022)
  • David S. Roh, Minor Transpacific: Triangulating American, Japanese, and Korean Fictions (2021)
  • Sunny Xiang, Tonal Intelligence: The Aesthetics of Asian Inscrutability During the Long Cold War (2020)
